## 副歌

堅定留下的忠心

沒有離去的念頭

Steadfast devotion to stay

Without temptation to stray



永遠是如此迅速

目的地只有一個

Always in motion so high

One destination to find

從空中俯瞰

與五花八門的鳥一起

Eyeing from the air

with one of every avian



底下的大地

和大地之上

down below at earth

and those upon



無數選擇中

無數噪音中

Out of such a choice,

out of all the noise



只有少數會

被他記得

Only few will be

in mind of one



只有少數會

Few can belong

## 主歌-2

是我的榮幸

能服務我的朋友

Pleasure's mine to be

in service of a friend to me



我唯一的夢想

以及使命

'Tis my only dream

and destiny



常常我覺得

如此不中用

Often times I feel

so inadequate



除了家人外

我還是什麼？

Who am I to you

but family?
